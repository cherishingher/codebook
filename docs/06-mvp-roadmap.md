# MVP Roadmap

## Phase 1: Business Loop

- Campus, user, student, guardian, teacher, and course models.
- Manual lesson creation.
- Teacher attendance confirmation.
- Deduction rules.
- Hour accounts and hour ledgers.
- Learner schedule and hour ledger views.

## Phase 2: Face Attendance

- Device heartbeat.
- Face enrollment.
- Face profile sync.
- USB camera recognition.
- Punch event upload.
- Automatic lesson matching.
- Idempotent attendance and deduction.

## Phase 3: Deployment

- HTTPS domain.
- Nginx reverse proxy.
- launchd service files.
- External disk backup.
- Device health checks.
- Permission and security tests.
